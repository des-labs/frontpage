Introducing the new DESaccess!

The DES Labs team at NCSA is proud to present the new and improved DESaccess service, providing you with the effective and feature-rich tools you need to access and utilize the Dark Energy Survey data. We are extending an invitation to you to begin your transition from the old DESaccess service to the new DESaccess service.

On November 12, 2020 the old site will be disabled and you will lose access to all job data generated via the old DESaccess service! Please download any generated data you wish to retain prior to that date.

Until then, both DESaccess sites will be online to allow for as smooth a transition as possible. Please contact us with questions or concerns using the help form.

Your username and password have not changed. If you have forgotten your password and/or your username, please use the new self-service password reset process on the login screen.
