DESaccess

After years of serving the needs of many researchers in the scientific community, the public DESaccess service has been retired. Please visit the service providers listed at the bottom of the page to explore the tools and services they offer for accessing Dark Energy Survey data.

DES collaboration members may continue using the private DESaccess service. Note: Job data is now only retained for 48 hours. Users are limited to 5 concurrent jobs. Cutout jobs may include up to 5000 positions each.

DES data releases documentation and related data files can be found here.

Visit the Dark Energy Survey home page for official news and information about the project.
